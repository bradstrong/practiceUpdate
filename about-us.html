ABOUT US

Why PracticeUpdate?

PracticeUpdate's mission is to efficiently provide busy healthcare professionals with unbiased expert-selected current information that they need to stay ahead. In many cases, our world-renowned experts provide insights and commentaries on the latest developments.

Our content is presented in a way that allows you to get the most for your time. You can quickly identify key new developments and efficiently engage with our various layers—Take Home Messages, expert commentaries, journal abstracts and full text articles—depending on your needs and interests.

Our technology allows you to view this content on any size screen so you can engage with PracticeUpdate on a computer, tablet or smartphone, regardless of the manufacturer, operating system, or browser (except Blackberry—not our fault).

No Bias, No Conflicts

No editorial content on PracticeUpdate is influenced, in any way, by commercial sponsors or interests. PracticeUpdate maintains the highest level of academic rigor, objectivity, and fair balance associated with all Elsevier products. We are commercially supported by online advertising and educational grants. Individual access to PracticeUpdate is free.

We Read Everything So You Don’t Have To

  1. The doctors on the PracticeUpdate team review the top journals, medical conference presentations and other credible peer-reviewed sources to identify developments that could impact your practice.
  2. Our world-renowned editorial board, in consultation with their colleagues on our advisory board, select the ‘must know’ information from these selections.
  3. In many cases, our board members or outside experts will provide insights and context to the developments in the form of commentaries. For broader topics of current interest, these experts create Expert Opinion pieces- which may be interviews (both text and video), seminal articles, FAQ pieces or conference commentaries.

In addition, our experts identify the key developments likely to be presented at major conferences and notify our members before the conference. Our conference coverage is designed to provide the specialist with both news (from respected third party sources) as well as analysis (from our experts) in a timely and efficient manner.
